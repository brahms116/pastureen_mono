What is this?

This is an honest and humble attempt at making a design system for pastureen. I am currently hoping to use this system across my personal projects to enable them to have a consistent look and feel to them. I honestly believe that this will require multiple iterations of design, but I am committed to seeing this through. As this is a hobby project, many of the important design system elements will be missing initially or just "not yet" documented. As elements and themes become clearer, I will be adding them to this site.
